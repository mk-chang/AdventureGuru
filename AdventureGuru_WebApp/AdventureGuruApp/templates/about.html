The Vision.

Of the 1.4 billion travelling in 2019, most of them know what to expect when purchasing an airline ticket or hotel room, but less when it comes to experience bookings and planning. The is because researching expeiences can be diffucult. Often times, the best places aren't a popular or time is wasted on a must-see desitination that actually isn't for you. Adventure Guru aims to enhance the travelling experience by using ML to offer personalized suggestions for travel destinations. This way less time is wasted researching and more time is spent travelling.

"In the 12 weeks leading up to a trip, there are 3x more experiences searches than hotel searches and 8x more experiences searches than air searches."
Think with Google, Greenberg Research

The Vision.

Getting away from the couch to travelling is beneficial for both the mind and soul. That fact is ubiquitous and more people are travelling. However, researching where best to visit can be diffucult. Often times, the best places are left out or time is wasted on a must-see desitination that just isn't for you. Adventure Guru aims to enhance the travelling experience by using AI to offer personalized suggestions for travel destinations. This way less time is wasted researching and more time is spent travelling.

My Story.

I grew up in Vancouver, but just recently graduated from University of Toronto with a Mech Eng degree. I focused on studying robotics and that's where I discovered my interest in developing software. My goal is to combine my passion for mathematics and writing code to solve everyday problems and replace outdated technologies. On my free time, I like to cook fusion cuisine, hike, attend festivals, and obviously, travel the world. After traveling on my grad trip, I realized that there was so many places that I had missed and that's when the idea of Adventure Guru is born.
